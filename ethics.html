Ethics & Governance

Qori Labs operates as a non-commercial lab. All work is currently conceptual or restricted to lab environments.

Consent (FPIC Vivo)

We operate under the standard of Free, Prior, and Informed Consent (FPIC). However, we go further with "FPIC vivo"â€”consent must be continuous, revocable, and verifiable by the community at any moment, not just a one-time signature.

Commercial Firewall

Qori Labs is a non-commercial research initiative. Any future licensing involving community knowledge must be co-designed with communities, governed by their institutions, and strictly protected from extractive market pressures.

Reciprocal Intelligence Safeguards

We translate ethical concerns into enforceable technical guarantees:

  • Governance: Oversight by legitimate Community AI Councils.
  • Data Sovereignty: CARE Principles applied to all data lifecycles.
  • Ultimate Control: Community-held technical kill switches.
  • Reciprocity: Mandatory value return (Ayni) recorded on ledgers.

Global Alignment

Our work builds upon and respects:

  • CARE Principles for Indigenous Data Governance
  • UNDRIP & ILO 169 (Self-determination)
  • Indigenous AI Protocols (Concordia/Abundant Intelligences)
  • Decolonial Computing and Relational Ethics
