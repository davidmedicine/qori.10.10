HigherSelf Protocol

A community-governed framework that encodes relational ethics into artificial intelligence.

Core Principles

Ayni (Reciprocity)

Technical translation: Reciprocal data flows with community benefit sharing. Data contributions must return value through accessible insights and collective decision power.

Minka (Collective Work)

Technical translation: Participatory design where community members are co-researchers, not subjects. Systems must amplify collective intelligence rather than replace human judgment.

Amaru (Serpent Wisdom)

Technical translation: Cyclical learning that adapts through ongoing community feedback. Knowledge is relational and contextual—not extractable or universal.
