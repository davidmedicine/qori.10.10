Qori Labs is a living laboratory for reciprocal intelligence—where Andean wisdom and ethical AI converge to serve community wellbeing.

Indigenous Sovereignty

In respectful dialogue with local communities in Cusco about how emerging technologies might support community wellbeing, cultural continuity, and self-determined futures—guided by Andean principles of Ayni and Minka. We invite other communities and institutions who share these values to join the conversation.

Core Framework

All work is guided by HigherSelf.ai, an open-source governance protocol that encodes Andean values of Yachay (right knowing), Llank’ay (right doing), and Munay (right loving).
