What is Reciprocal Intelligence?

Reciprocal Intelligence (RI) is a proposed operational standard for AI and agent systems in Indigenous territories. It translates CARE, UNDRIP and Andean reciprocity into concrete governance and technical requirements. Read the RI Framework

Core Properties

  • Reciprocity as Obligation: Systems must return tangible value (Ayni) to the territory.
  • Community Authority as Law: Local assemblies hold binding power over system operation.
  • Offline-First Infrastructure: Systems run on community-controlled hardware, not the cloud.

Current Research Focus

The Standard

Governance R&D. Developing the legal and technical protocols (Value Return Agreements) that make "Ayni" enforceable in code.

The Tech

The Qori Node. Engineering a unified, solar-powered communication node that runs offline AI and SMS services on local silicon.

The Action

Pilot Validation. We are seeking mission-aligned universities and industrial sponsors to stress-test our infrastructure in high-altitude environments.
